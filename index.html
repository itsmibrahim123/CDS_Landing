The Problem

Manual review is slow, inconsistent, and hard to audit.

Property financing in Pakistan relies on labour-intensive legal review of sale deeds, mutations, and allotment letters. Banks need standardised outputs — but no two law firms deliver the same format.

  • ⏱
    Time-consuming review cycles Legal review often spans days per case, creating financing bottlenecks.
  • ⚠
    Inconsistent output formats Each firm produces varied formats with limited audit traceability.
  • 🔎
    Human error in risk detection Chain-of-title gaps and encumbrances are easy to miss manually.
  • 🏛
    No regulatory audit trail Banks cannot trace how review decisions were reached or by whom.

Core Capabilities

Everything a legal team needs, automated.

From raw document upload to a signed bank pack — CDS handles every step of the diligence pipeline.

📄
OCR with Confidence Scoring

Per-page confidence scores using Tesseract and PaddleOCR. Every extraction is logged and traceable for audit purposes.

🗂
Automated Classification

Documents are automatically classified — sale deeds, mutations, allotment letters — and routed to the right extraction pipeline.

⚙
Configurable Rules Engine

Detect missing documents, risk keywords, ownership mismatches, and chain-of-title gaps against banking and legal standards.

📋
CP Generation

Automatically produce Conditions Precedent aligned with bank review practices — reducing legal team drafting time significantly.

✍
Draft Assistance

Auto-generate discrepancy letters, undertakings, indemnities, and legal opinion skeletons from structured case data.

📦
Bank Pack Export

One-click export of an executive summary, exceptions list, CP list, and document annexures — in DOCX and PDF.
